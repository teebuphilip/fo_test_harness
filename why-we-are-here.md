# Why We Are Here

This repository began as a test harness for `~/Documents/work/FounderOps`, built to validate the rules and specifications in `docs/architecture/INTAKE` and `docs/architecture/BUILD`. I needed a reliable way to generate builds and test against them while FounderOps was still being completed.

Along the way, priorities shifted. With AI advancing rapidly over the next 12–18 months and an urgent need for revenue, this harness has become the primary vehicle for near‑term results.

In parallel, `~/Documents/work/AFH` (AutoFounderHub) houses a git workflow that produces small, practical SaaS ideas aimed at small businesses, not enterprises. Ideas are scored, and the best are sent here to be built. I will also be taking builds from personal networks as they lead to larger opportunities.

AFH will become a full productized website that sells ideas, scoring, complete working codebases, and deployable turnkey solutions. Pricing tiers are planned at $2.99, $5.99, $2,500, and $10,000, with feature work priced separately after purchase.

Target launch is May 15 (immediately after business tax filing), or a nearby date that avoids tax deadlines. Before then, I intend to complete **60 builds** and deploy them to my own Vercel and Railway accounts. The `agent-make/` directory lists the 60 planned builds. I am already working on three higher‑complexity builds for myself and close collaborators: **wynwood**, **awi**, and **aav**.

The immediate goal is to tighten the procedures around intake, build, and QA:
- stronger requirements,
- fewer hallucinations,
- clearer, higher‑quality QA.

Finally, I will bring in basketball‑related projects from `~/Documents/work/courtdominion` and run them through this engine as part of the same factory.

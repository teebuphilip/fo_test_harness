# Founder Questionnaire - Answer Template

Please answer the following 10 questions about your startup idea in as much detail as possible.
Be specific, honest, and thorough. These answers will be used to generate your intake evaluation.

---

1. What problem are we solving?

[Your detailed answer here - explain the problem, who has it, and why it matters]


2. Who is our primary customer?

[Describe your target users/customers - be specific about segments, demographics, needs]


3. What is the simplest version of our product that still delivers value?

[Describe your MVP - what's the minimum you need to ship to solve the problem]


4. What actions should our users be able to take?

[List the key user actions - what should they be able to do with your product]


5. What inputs do we need from the user?

[What information/data do you need from users to deliver value]


6. What outputs do we deliver back to them?

[What value/outputs do users receive from your product]


7. Do we need external integrations or data sources?

[List any third-party services, APIs, data sources you'll need]


8. Are payments or transactions involved?

[Describe your revenue model - how will you make money]


9. What does success look like in the first 30 days?

[Be specific - what do you want to achieve in month 1]


10. What constraints or non-goals must we respect?

[What are your limits? What do you want to AVOID doing? What won't you compromise on?]


---

HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE:

1. Save this file as: hero_text/your_startup_name_MM_DD_YYYY.txt
2. Answer all 10 questions in detail
3. Run: python convert_hero_answers.py hero_text/your_startup_name_MM_DD_YYYY.txt
4. Review the generated JSON
5. Continue with: ./generate_intake.sh your_startup_name.json

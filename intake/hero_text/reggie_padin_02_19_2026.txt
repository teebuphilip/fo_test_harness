
1    What problem are you trying to solve?

    Organizations lack a centralized intelligence system that connects AI adoption, workforce development, and measurable business performance. Learning investments are measured operationally (courses completed, hours delivered) rather than economically (productivity, revenue contribution, talent velocity). The product solves the absence of a structured AI Workforce Intelligence platform that quantifies readiness, identifies KPI gaps, and forecasts business impact.

2    Who is the primary user or customer for this solution?

    Primary user: AI Learning Consultants, fractional CLOs, workforce transformation advisors.

    Economic buyer: CHROs, CLOs, COOs, private equity operators, and executive teams seeking measurable AI workforce performance visibility.

 6   What output does the system return to the user?

    AI Workforce Readiness Score (0–100)

    KPI gap analysis across five core development metrics

		a. Time to Competency (TTC)

		Definition:
		Average number of days from employee start (or role change) to reaching defined productivity threshold.

		Data Required:
			•	Employee start date
			•	Role start date
			•	Date performance threshold met

		Output:
			•	Org average TTC
			•	Department average TTC
			•	Trend over time

		b. Performance Delta Post-Training (PDPT)

		Definition:
		Percentage improvement in a defined performance metric after training.

		Formula:
		(Post Metric – Pre Metric) ÷ Pre Metric

		Data Required:
			•	Pre-training KPI value
			•	Post-training KPI value
			•	Training completion date

		Output:
			•	% improvement
			•	Aggregate average by program
			•	Program effectiveness ranking

		c. Revenue per Learner (RPL)

		Definition:
		Revenue generated or influenced per trained employee.

		Formula:
		Revenue Attributed ÷ Number of Trained Employees

		Data Required:
			•	Revenue data (team or individual level)
			•	Training participation list

		Output:
			•	Revenue per learner
			•	Revenue uplift vs non-trained group

		d. Behavioral Change Index (BCI)

		Definition:
		Manager-rated improvement score 30–90 days post training.

		Input:
		1–5 rating scale across defined competencies.

		Output:
			•	Average behavior improvement score
			•	Improvement distribution
			•	Correlation with performance delta

		e. Internal Mobility Velocity (IMV)

		Definition:
		Percentage of roles filled internally within defined time period.

		Formula:
		Internal Promotions ÷ Total Roles Filled

		Output:
			•	Mobility rate
			•	Succession readiness indicator
			•	Year-over-year change







    Risk exposure classification (Low / Moderate / High)

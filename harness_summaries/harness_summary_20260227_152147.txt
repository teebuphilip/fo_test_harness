Title: Building 70 SaaS products to stress-test my AI build harness — 5 down, 65 to go
Body:
I built a deterministic SaaS harness with 53 capabilities (auth, payments, fraud detection, GDPR compliance, the works). Now I need to know if it actually works at scale.
So I'm building 70 different SaaS products through it. Real FastAPI + React + Stripe deployments. If the harness breaks, I fix it. If it holds, I know it's solid.

What I learned:
- High failure rates in Adversarial AI Validator and Wynwood Thoroughbreds due to CTRL_C and BUILD_TRUNCATED issues.
- Ai Workforce Intelligence showed significant cost spikes, peaking at $5.4001 during a non-converging run.
- Average costs varied significantly; Freelance Invoice Tracker had the highest average at $1.65 with only 2 runs.
- Convergence issues are critical; 15 iterations failed to yield results in Ai Workforce Intelligence.
- Scalability concerns arise with frequent CTRL_C failures across multiple products, indicating resource limits.

Following this for the next 6 months. Will post failures and edge cases as I find them.

What I learned:

- CTRL_C is the most common failure mode, indicating potential issues with resource limits or timeouts.
- Ai Workforce Intelligence shows significant scalability issues, with costs rising sharply in larger runs.

- Non-converging runs often exceed 10 iterations, indicating a need for better convergence strategies.

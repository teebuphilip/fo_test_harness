Title: Building 70 SaaS products to stress-test my AI build harness — 5 down, 65 to go
Body:
I built a deterministic SaaS harness with 53 capabilities (auth, payments, fraud detection, GDPR compliance, the works). Now I need to know if it actually works at scale.
So I'm building 70 different SaaS products through it. Real FastAPI + React + Stripe deployments. If the harness breaks, I fix it. If it holds, I know it's solid.

What I learned:
- AI Workforce Intelligence showed high costs ($5.40) with non-convergence at 15 iterations, indicating scalability issues.
- Frequent CTRL_C failures across multiple products suggest instability in the testing environment or resource limitations.
- Wynwood Thoroughbreds had a high failure rate (40%) due to build truncation, highlighting potential integration issues.
- Average costs varied significantly across products, indicating inconsistent resource utilization and optimization opportunities.

Title: Building 70 SaaS products to stress-test my AI build harness — 5 down, 65 to go
Body:
I built a deterministic SaaS harness with 53 capabilities (auth, payments, fraud detection, GDPR compliance, the works). Now I need to know if it actually works at scale.
So I'm building 70 different SaaS products through it. Real FastAPI + React + Stripe deployments. If the harness breaks, I fix it. If it holds, I know it's solid.

What I learned:
- High failure rates in AI Workforce Intelligence indicate significant convergence issues, especially over 15 iterations.
- Adversarial AI Validator consistently fails with CTRL_C, suggesting a critical stability problem in execution.
- Total costs for AI Workforce Intelligence spike significantly during non-converging runs, indicating inefficiency.
- Wynwood Thoroughbreds shows frequent build truncation, highlighting potential scalability issues in deployment.
- Average costs vary minimally across products, suggesting a need for cost optimization strategies.
